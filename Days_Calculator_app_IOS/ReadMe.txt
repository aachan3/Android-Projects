Instructions

To calculate the days between any two dates, please enter the following accordingly in the interface:

1)Enter the values in numbers accordingy to the month, day,year specifications.Ex:(MM/DD/YYYY) 

2)Click on Calculate to find the number of days.

* Days and Months can be represented with 01 or 1(for january)for example.

This APP validates for:

->Start Date Greater than End Date

->Any illegal Expressions or characters in fields

->Dates according to months and leap years

->Empty Values.
